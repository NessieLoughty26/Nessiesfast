Tips & Tricks 💡
Science-backed guidance for a beautiful fast
Getting started
💧
Hydration is everything
Drink 2–3 litres of water during your fast. Add a pinch of sea salt or electrolyte drops to prevent headaches and fatigue, especially in the first few days.
☕
Black coffee & tea are your allies
Plain black coffee and unsweetened teas don't break a fast. They can even boost autophagy and make the fasting window feel much more manageable.
🌙
Leverage your sleep
Start your fast a few hours before bed so you sleep through the hardest hours. You'll wake up already halfway through — total game changer.
Breaking your fast
🥑
Break gently, always
After a long fast, avoid jumping straight to a big meal. Start with something light — a small salad, broth, or a few nuts — before your main meal 30 minutes later.
🍋
Lemon water trick
A glass of warm water with lemon juice 15 minutes before breaking your fast prepares your digestive system and keeps blood sugar stable.
🥗
Prioritise protein first
Eat protein at your first meal to support muscle retention and keep you satisfied longer. Eggs, legumes, fish, and Greek yogurt are all excellent choices.
Mindset & lifestyle
🧘
Hunger is a wave, not a wall
Hunger peaks and passes, usually within 20–30 minutes. Drink water, go for a short walk, or do a breathing exercise — it almost always fades on its own.
📅
Consistency over perfection
Missing one day is not failure. What matters is the pattern over weeks, not one imperfect day. Be gentle with yourself and simply begin again.
🎯
Know your why
Write down what you want to achieve — better energy, mental clarity, metabolic health. Returning to your why on hard days is more powerful than any willpower trick.
